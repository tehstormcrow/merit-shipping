Our Mission

Our mission is to provide shipowners and all maritime stakeholders with high-quality, sustainable services in full compliance with international standards.

Ship Management

We offer comprehensive management services for vessels of all types and sizes. Our team ensures smooth operations through effective planning and 24/7 support.

Crew Management

We supply qualified, experienced, and certified seafarers for all vessel types. Our crew management process is transparent, reliable, and fully compliant with MLC standards.

Technical Management

We provide full technical management services, covering maintenance, repairs, spare parts procurement, and performance monitoring, delivering professional solutions for every vessel’s needs.

Vessel Sales & Purchase

We support clients in buying and selling new or second-hand vessels with reliable market analysis, valuation, and professional brokerage services

Maritime Consultancy

We offer tailored consultancy services to shipowners and investors, guiding them on operational efficiency, investment planning, and a wide range of maritime strategies.

Shipyard & Maintenance Management

We professionally handle the planning and oversight of ship maintenance and repair projects, including shipyard selection, cost optimization, and full process monitoring.

Survey Services

We provide independent surveys in accordance with international standards, including vessel valuation, condition assessment, pre-class inspections, and other survey requirements.
